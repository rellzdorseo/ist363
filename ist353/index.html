Who We Are

About Us

Our Story
Built From the Community, For the Community

J.M.D. E.N.T. INCORP. started as a grassroots initiative — a handful of coaches, a community gymnasium, and a shared vision: give every child access to quality sports programming regardless of background or income.

What began as weekend pickup games grew into a full-fledged organization offering structured leagues across four sports, serving hundreds of youth athletes across multiple communities.

Today, we are committed to expanding our reach, enhancing our programs, and continuing to build the next generation of champions — on and off the field.

Participation Waiver
Terms & Conditions

By registering your child with J.M.D. E.N.T. INCORP., you acknowledge and agree to the following terms: participants take part in all activities voluntarily and at their own risk. J.M.D. E.N.T. INCORP. takes all reasonable precautions to ensure athlete safety.

Parents/guardians grant permission for their child to participate in sports activities, travel to and from events, and receive emergency medical treatment if necessary. Photography and video may be taken at events for organizational purposes.

For full waiver documentation, please contact us at info@jmdent.org or visit our office during business hours.
